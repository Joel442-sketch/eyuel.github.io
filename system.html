Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

Browse the courses page, select a course, and click "Enroll". You'll need to be logged in as a student.

Can I access courses on mobile?

Yes! Our platform is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, or contact support for assistance.

Are there any free courses?

Yes, we offer several free courses. Look for courses marked as "Free" on the courses page.
